3D floor plans are enhanced and more appealing substitute to 2D floor plans

VISUALLY EXPERIENCE YOUR FLOOR PLANS

3D floor plans are enhanced and more appealing substitute to 2D floor plans. It helps the prospective buyers to better understand the specifications and interior layout of the floor in 3D. Above all, the options are immense when it comes to showcasing the specific areas. Choose either angled or top views for your property layout to present it better.

DEFINE THE CHARACTER OF THE SPACE

Unlike simple 2D black & white or coloured floor plans, 2D textured plans showcase the physical space with added elements that communicate the character of that space. HIGHLIGHT DETAILS THAT MATTER

Visually engaging, the 3D floor plans created by Benchmark Studio are the perfect marketing tool for new build properties or projects which require an additional bit of detail. With 3D floor plans, you can take complete control of what areas you want to highlight the most.

FULLY CUSTOMIZABLE

The only limitation is the imagination in 3D floor plans. Customize them all you want - change the colours, play with the angles, illustrate floor finishes, add furniture or a few props to give the sense of scale and lifestyle. Moreover, with the help of interior specifications, 3D floor plan will speak for itself.
