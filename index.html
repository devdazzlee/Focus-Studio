FLOOR PLANS

Floor plans serve as invaluable tools for presenting the layout of commercial, residential, or industrial buildings. These architectural drawings offer a comprehensive overview, aiding potential buyers in assessing whether a property aligns with their requirements by accurately showcasing available space, essential features, and overall design.

PHOTO EDITING

Photos and images play a crucial role in providing initial impressions of properties. They offer perspectives of real estate, bridging the gap for individuals not familiar with technical floor plan terminology. Our diverse photo editing solutions enhance clarity and understanding, ensuring impactful visual representation.

VIDEO EDITING

With decades of experience in video editing, Home Focus Studio caters to diverse clientele, transforming raw footage into polished videos efficiently. Our services include merging scenes, arranging footage, enhancing quality, and adding creative elements to produce captivating videos across various formats.

GRAPHIC DESIGNS

Dynamic visual content significantly influences consumer behavior. Our graphic design services empower your marketing efforts, attracting and engaging audiences effectively. Let your business stand out with compelling designs that resonate with your target audience.

VIRTUAL TOURS

Experience properties from the comfort of your home with our immersive 360° tours. Explore any desired property virtually, eliminating the need for physical visits. With Home focus studio, discover your dream property effortlessly.

VIRTUAL STAGING

Maximize the appeal of your property with professional virtual staging services. Present your property in the best light, attracting potential buyers with vibrant and inviting visuals. Enhance online listings and captivate your audience effortlessly.

MATTERPORT SERVICES

Transform physical spaces into immersive 3D digital environments with our Matterport services. Our experienced team utilizes cutting-edge technology to create accurate representations of properties, providing a comprehensive viewing experience for potential buyers.

BIM SERVICES

Unlock the potential of Building Information Modeling (BIM) for your architectural and construction projects. Our comprehensive BIM services encompass planning, design, construction, and maintenance, providing detailed insights to optimize project efficiency and client satisfaction.

CGI / ARTIST IMPRESSIONS

Immerse your audience in conceptual designs with CGI and artist impressions. Whether it's architectural visualization or landscape depiction, our artistic renderings bring ideas to life, enabling clients to envision projects with clarity and precision.
